Frequently Asked Questions

Is the extension really free?

Yes, completely free for personal use. No trials, no feature gates, no credit card required. Companies and teams must purchase a Teams license.

Do I still need Xcode?

Yes. FlowDeck uses Apple's build tools under the hood. You need Xcode installed, but you won't need to open it.

What editors are supported?

VS Code, Cursor, Windsurf, and any VS Code fork. Search for "FlowDeck" in the Extensions view.

What's the difference from the CLI?

The extension gives you IDE integration. The CLI adds terminal workflows, AI agent integration, and automation for power users.
